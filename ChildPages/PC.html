Pc Controls

  • To jump you press 'Space'
  • To sneak or crouch, you press 'shift'
  • To move, use the keys 'WASD' stick
  • To look around, use the right thumbstick
  • To open the crafting menu/inventory, press I
  • To open the in game menu, press the 'Esc key'
  • To Quit game press quit game at the main interface
  • To switch between tools, weapons, and other items use the the top number keys on your keyboard or use the mouse scroll wheel
  • To place a block or to use/eat an item (press right click on mouse )
  • To attack or break a block,(press left click on mouse))
  • (These controls can all be changed to a preferable layout through the controls menu)
