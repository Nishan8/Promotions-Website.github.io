Console controls
  • To jump you press 'A' on Xbox(Or the equivalent according on your console)
  • To sneak or crouch, you press 'B' on Xbox(Or the equivalent on your console)
  • To move, use the left thumb stick
  • To look around, use the right thumbstick
  • To open the crafting menu/inventory, press Y
  • To open the in game menu, press the button on the right to the middle on the controller
  • To open the Xbox menu press the X on the middle top of the controller
  • To switch between tools, weapons, and other items use the top bumper buttons, LB and RB
  • To place a block or to use/eat an item, press Left trigger(LT)
  • To attack or break a block, press Right trigger(RT)
  • (These controls can all be changed to a preferable layout)
