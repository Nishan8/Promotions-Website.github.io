Survival Mode

Survival Mode is a gamemode which allows you to play in a world where you have to find resources and use them to build, complete achievements etc.

This makes you limited to breaking certain blocks and having to collect resources by yourself.

To go on Survival mode, type the command "/gamemode survival" in chat (chat is accesed by t)
